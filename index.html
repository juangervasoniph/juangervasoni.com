Hi, I'm Juan

What I actually do

I'm a digital marketing specialist based in Buenos Aires. For the past 6+ years I've worked embedded with companies internationally as their sole or lead marketer, building the strategy, running the campaigns, and setting up the systems that keep everything moving without needing to hire a full team.

At GrowthMarketers, I built the full marketing operation for one of the leaders in relief veterinary staffing in the US: programmatic SEO, Google and Meta Ads, email automation, content, and automated reporting. Monthly leads grew 10x in 12 months. I also built a cold outreach base of over 15,000 veterinarians and 5,000+ US vet clinics.

Before that, I ran content strategy and creative production for BetterVet, the second-largest mobile vet service in the US, including directing their national TV commercial remotely from Argentina.

I work across the full stack: strategy, execution, and the automation layer that ties it all together.
