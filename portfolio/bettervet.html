The brief.

BetterVet needed a consistent social media video presence that could educate pet owners, build trust in the brand, and grow organic reach across multiple platforms simultaneously. The challenge was producing high-quality content at volume without losing the authentic, human feel that resonates in the pet care space.

Every piece of content had to serve two goals at once: stop the scroll and communicate credibility. In a space where trust is everything, that balance mattered.

What we did.

We developed a full content strategy built around educational short-form video, behind-the-scenes vet content, and performance-driven social ads. Editing style was adapted per platform — faster cuts and trending formats for TikTok, more polished storytelling for Instagram, and community-focused content for Facebook.

All video was produced and edited in-house, which meant faster turnaround, tighter brand consistency, and creative decisions made with performance in mind from the first frame.
